# Reasoning Evaluation Rubric

This rubric measures the quality of an AI model's reasoning rather than simply whether the final answer is correct.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Score |
|-----------|:----:|
| Logical Consistency | /5 |
| Step-by-Step Reasoning | /5 |
| Internal Consistency | /5 |
| Assumption Quality | /5 |
| Final Conclusion | /5 |

---

## Common Failures

- Logical contradiction
- Invalid assumptions
- Missing reasoning steps
- Circular reasoning
- False dilemma
- Non sequitur
- Unsupported conclusion
- Arithmetic errors
- Reasoning that reaches the correct answer by accident

---

## Reviewer Questions

- Does the reasoning follow logically?
- Are assumptions justified?
- Are intermediate steps correct?
- Does the conclusion follow from the evidence?
- Would another evaluator reach the same conclusion?
